Industrial Steam Cleaner Applications: 8 Industry Use Cases

By Guangdong Jieneng 2026-07-16
← Back to Blog

Industrial steam cleaners go far beyond simple parts washing. This article surveys 8 industries with real application examples.

1. Automotive Manufacturing

Auto components accumulate cutting fluid, rust-preventive oil, and metal chips during processing. Jieneng's automated shock-absorber cylinder cleaning machine handles auto-loading, rough wash, fine wash, rust prevention, water displacement, drying, and auto-unloading — with 10-minute fixture changeover. Engine blocks, crankshafts, and camshafts cleaned with 170-210°C high-pressure steam achieve ISO 16232 cleanliness standards.

2. Machining: Mold and Tool Cleaning

Molds in injection and die-casting accumulate carbon, resin, and oil deposits. Traditional cleaning requires 4-8 hours of disassembly and soaking. Steam cleaning can be performed in-line: high-temperature steam directly blasts mold surfaces, completing cleaning in 30-60 minutes without disassembly. CNC machines and stamping equipment also benefit from steam cleaning for routine maintenance, reducing downtime.

3. Electronics Manufacturing

Electronic components require extremely high cleanliness. Water washing risks short circuits; chemical solvents may corrode solder joints. Low-pressure dry steam (140-170°C, 4-6 bar) is ideal — steam leaves no moisture, doesn't corrode components, and penetrates micro-crevices to remove flux residue and dust. The Jieneng JNX-6 is deployed in multiple electronics factories' PCB cleaning lines.

4. Chemical and Oil & Gas: Safe Cleaning in Explosive Environments

Chemical plant equipment surfaces accumulate chemical residues, oil, and dust. Standard cleaning equipment poses explosion risks in hazardous areas. The Jieneng JNX-EX20 explosion-proof steam cleaner carries ATEX + EAC dual certification, delivering 20 MPa high pressure and 220°C steam for safe operation in Zone 1 and Zone 2 areas. Suitable for reactor exterior cleaning, pipeline flange degreasing, and pump cleaning.

5. Food Processing: Chemical-Free Sanitary Cleaning

Food machinery requires chemical-residue-free cleaning. Steam cleaning uses pure water vapor — 170°C provides natural sanitization without any chemicals, meeting food-grade cleanliness standards. Suitable for conveyor belts, mixers, packaging machines, and storage tanks.

6. Rail Transit

Train braking systems, bogies, and wheels accumulate oil, rust, and brake dust. The Jieneng JNX-10 rail maintenance steam cleaner is purpose-built for rail workshops: 10 kW, 6-10 bar, 170-210°C steam, stainless steel body for 24-hour continuous operation, PLC-controlled preset cleaning programs.

7. New Energy: Battery and PV Component Cleaning

In lithium battery production, electrode coating and slitting equipment require regular cleaning to prevent cross-contamination. Low-pressure steam removes electrolyte residue without damaging coatings. For solar panels, the Jieneng JNX-L crawler-type PV panel cleaning robot features dry-brush/water-wash dual modes, 15° climbing capability, and 24V lithium battery with 3-4 hour runtime.

8. Marine and Offshore

Ship and offshore platform equipment faces salt spray, marine organisms, and oil contamination. High-pressure steam effectively removes salt crystals and oil without damaging anti-corrosion coatings. Jieneng's 304 stainless steel models offer excellent corrosion resistance for long-term marine use.
