Industrial Steam Cleaner Maintenance Guide: 3-Year Warranty Care

By Guangdong Jieneng 2026-07-16
← Back to Blog

Buying equipment is just the first step — using it effectively is the real challenge. This guide details maintenance priorities during the 3-year warranty period.

1. Daily Maintenance (5 Minutes)

  1. Check water tank level — use softened or purified water (hard water causes scale)
  2. Inspect power cord and steam hoses for damage
  3. Verify pressure and temperature gauges after startup
  4. Drain remaining water after work to prevent scale buildup
  5. Clean nozzle filter screen to prevent clogging

Using softened water is the single most important daily habit — hard water scale is the #1 failure source for steam cleaners.

2. Weekly Maintenance (15 Minutes)

  1. Check high-pressure pump oil level; top up with专用 pump oil if needed
  2. Drain sediment from steam generator drain valve
  3. Inspect all connections for leaks (joints, flanges, seals)
  4. Clean exterior casing and cooling fan filter
  5. Test safety functions: manually trigger over-pressure relief valve

3. Monthly Maintenance (30 Minutes)

  1. Check and calibrate temperature and pressure sensors
  2. Inspect electrical terminals for looseness (power off)
  3. Verify PLC touchscreen responsiveness and program integrity
  4. Check nozzle wear (wear causes pressure drop and angle change)
  5. Record operating data; maintain a service log

4. Quarterly Maintenance (1 Hour)

  1. Change high-pressure pump oil (first change at 500 hours, then quarterly)
  2. Inspect steam generator boiler for scale (descale if needed)
  3. Check safety valve and pressure vessel seals
  4. Inspect contactors and relays in electrical control box
  5. Schedule remote diagnostics or on-site inspection with manufacturer

5. Annual Major Service (Manufacturer-Led)

  1. Comprehensive performance testing of steam generator, pump, controls
  2. Replace wear parts: seals, O-rings, filter cartridges, nozzles
  3. Calibrate all sensors and gauges
  4. Upgrade PLC software (if new version available)
  5. Issue annual equipment health report

7. Key to Longevity: Good Water + Regular Descaling

Limescale is the steam cleaner's greatest enemy. In hard water areas (>200 mg/L hardness), a water softener is mandatory — otherwise heating elements burn out in 3-6 months. Install a pre-softener keeping hardness below 50 mg/L. Existing scale can be removed with citric acid circulation cleaning. Jieneng provides专用 descaling agent and technical guidance.
