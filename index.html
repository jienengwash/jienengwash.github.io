Why Choose Jieneng

As a National High-Tech and Specialized & Sophisticated Enterprise, we combine certified quality with engineering flexibility to serve industrial buyers worldwide.

Direct Manufacturer

Source from the factory, not a trading company. Full in-house engineering enables non-standard customization for any industrial cleaning requirement.

ATEX & EAC Dual Certification

EU ATEX (2014/34/EU) and Eurasian EAC (TR CU 012/2011) explosion-proof certifications. Explosion-proof configuration and certificate documents can be provided for project review when applicable.

24-Hour Continuous Operation

Italian AR high-pressure plunger pump delivers stable, uninterrupted performance around the clock — rated for continuous duty in production environments.

PLC + 10" Touch Screen

Programmable logic controller with an intuitive 10-inch color touchscreen. Operators can set parameters, monitor status, and adjust steam output in real time.

High-Temp Steam, Low Water Use

Steam output at 160–220 °C cuts through heavy grease and oil while consuming up to 80% less water than conventional wash-down methods.

Quick-Change Fixtures

Automated fixture system switches between workpiece types in under 10 minutes. One machine, multiple applications — reducing capital expenditure.

Soft Water & Dry-Wet Adjustable

Built-in water softening prevents scale buildup. Steam dryness is continuously adjustable from wet to dry, adapting to different cleaning tasks.
