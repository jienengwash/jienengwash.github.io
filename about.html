About Jieneng

From a specialized manufacturer in Shantou to a nationally certified industrial steam cleaning equipment supplier — delivering engineered solutions for demanding environments worldwide.

Guangdong Jieneng Electrical Technology Co., Ltd.

Founded in Shantou, Guangdong Province, Jieneng is a leading manufacturer of industrial steam cleaning equipment in China. As a National High-Tech Enterprise and Specialized & Sophisticated Enterprise, we design and build steam cleaning systems for industries where performance, safety, and reliability are non-negotiable.

Our product portfolio spans explosion-proof steam cleaners certified to EU ATEX and Eurasian EAC standards, precision parts cleaning systems for automotive and manufacturing, steam car wash machines, crawler-type photovoltaic panel cleaning robots, and fully automated inline cleaning lines. Every machine is engineered in-house, giving us full control over quality and the ability to customize for non-standard requirements.

We serve clients across oil & gas, chemical processing, automotive manufacturing, steel processing, food production, and solar energy — both domestically and internationally.

9 Core Advantages

What sets Jieneng apart — from certified safety to engineering flexibility, every advantage is built into the machine.

Original Manufacturer with Full Non-Standard Customization

Source directly from the factory — not a trading company. In-house engineering team enables full non-standard customization for any industrial cleaning requirement, from chamber dimensions to steam output profiles.

ATEX + EAC Dual International Explosion-Proof Certification

EU ATEX (2014/34/EU) and Eurasian EAC (TR CU 012/2011) explosion-proof certifications. Explosion-proof configuration and certificate documents can be provided for project review when applicable.

Italian AR High-Pressure Plunger Pump — 24h Continuous Rated

The Italian AR high-pressure plunger pump delivers stable, uninterrupted performance around the clock — rated for continuous 24-hour duty in production environments without performance degradation.

PLC + 10-Inch Touch Screen Intelligent Control

Programmable logic controller with an intuitive 10-inch color touch screen. Operators can set parameters, monitor real-time status, and adjust steam output, temperature, and pressure on the fly.

High-Temperature Steam Saves 80% Water vs. Traditional Methods

Steam output at 160–220 °C cuts through heavy grease and oil while consuming up to 80% less water than conventional wash-down methods — lower operating costs and reduced wastewater treatment burden.

Automated Quick-Change Tooling — 10-Minute Changeover

Automated fixture system switches between workpiece types in under 10 minutes. One machine handles multiple applications — reducing capital expenditure and floor space requirements.

Soft Water Anti-Scale + Dry/Wet Steam Adjustable

Built-in water softening prevents scale buildup and extends equipment life. Steam dryness is continuously adjustable from wet to dry, adapting to different cleaning tasks and surface sensitivities.

Nationwide On-Site Service + 3-Year Warranty

Every machine ships with a 3-year warranty. Our service team provides nationwide on-site installation, operator training, and maintenance across China. Rapid response for critical production lines.
