JNX-10 Industrial Steam Cleaner for Rail Maintenance Workshop

10kW Dry Steam Cleaning Machine for Locomotive Underframe, Bogie & Brake System Cleaning

The JNX-10 is a 10kW electric dry steam cleaning machine designed for industrial maintenance and workshop cleaning applications. It is suitable for rail maintenance workshops where high-temperature steam is required to soften grease, oil film, carbon deposits, brake dust and industrial dirt on locomotive underframes, bogies, brake systems, bearings, engine bays and workshop floors. Steam cleaning helps reduce workshop flooding and wastewater volume compared with conventional high-volume water washing.
