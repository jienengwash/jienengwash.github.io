Tender Clarification Notes

Power Supply Specification

Some tender specifications may list 220–240V / 50Hz power supply and 2–5kW total power consumption. For true 10kW industrial steam output and continuous-duty workshop cleaning, 380V / 50Hz / 3-phase power supply is recommended. The 10kW steam generator cannot deliver rated output on single-phase 220–240V supply. Project site power availability should be confirmed before final configuration. If only 220–240V is available, a lower-output compact steam cleaner or customized solution should be discussed with the manufacturer.

Pressure Specification Clarification

Tender specifications referencing high-pressure water jet cleaning (e.g., 100–200 bar pressure washing) describe a fundamentally different cleaning mechanism. The JNX-10 uses dry steam at 6–10 bar — the cleaning effect comes from high-temperature steam dissolution (170–210°C), not mechanical water blast. Steam cleaning is generally more effective for grease, oil and carbon deposit removal while consuming 90–95% less water. If the tender specifically requires high-pressure water jet cleaning, a pressure washer should be sourced separately.

HS Code Confirmation

The suggested HS code 8424300000 (Steam or sand blasting machines and similar jet projecting machines) is based on the product classification for electric steam cleaning machines. Final HS code confirmation should be obtained from a licensed customs broker before shipment, as classification may vary by destination country.

Packing Dimensions

The packing dimensions listed (870 × 650 × 905 mm) are reference values. Final packing dimensions will be confirmed before shipment based on the actual configuration, optional modules included, and export packaging requirements. Wooden crate packaging is available for maritime shipment at additional cost.
